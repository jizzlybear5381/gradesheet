How is GPA calculated?

Grade point average weighs each course by its credit hours, not just the letter grade. A 4-credit course pulls your average further than a 1-credit course. The formula is:

GPA = (credit hours × grade points, summed across courses) ÷ total credit hours

Grade points typically follow a 4.0 scale: A = 4.0, A- = 3.7, B+ = 3.3, B = 3.0, and so on down to F = 0.0. Some schools use a 4.3 or 5.0 scale instead, so check your registrar's grading policy if your GPA needs to match an official transcript.
