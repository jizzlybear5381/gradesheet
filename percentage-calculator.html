The three formulas

X% of Y = (X ÷ 100) × Y — used for tips, discounts, or "what's 15% of 240".

X is what % of Y = (X ÷ Y) × 100 — used for test scores, like "36 correct out of 240".

Percentage change = ((Y − X) ÷ X) × 100 — positive means an increase, negative means a decrease.
