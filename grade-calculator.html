How the weighting works

Each category counts in proportion to its weight, not equally. The formula is:

Grade = Σ(weight × score) ÷ Σ(weight)

If your weights already add up to 100%, this is just a weighted average. If they don't — say your syllabus only lists three categories that sum to 90% — GradeSheet still normalizes correctly by dividing by the total weight you entered, so partial syllabi work fine too.

Common weight splits

  • Homework 20%, midterms 30%, final exam 35%, participation 15%
  • Labs 25%, quizzes 15%, project 20%, final exam 40%
  • Assignments 40%, midterm 20%, final 40%
